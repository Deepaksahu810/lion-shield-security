About Us

A trusted name in professional security and facility management — protecting what matters most.

Our Story & Mission

Lion Shield Security Services Pvt. Ltd. is a professionally managed private limited company dedicated to providing world-class security and facility management solutions across India. We carry on the business of security services, security guards, bodyguards, and national-level guarding services including event security, elite services, industrial security, personnel security, cash escort services, electronic security surveillance, manned guarding, detective services, and facility management.

In the era of globalization, geographic boundaries are shrinking faster than ever — necessitating the movement of people and assets across different areas. Economic growth has created a heightened need for security across all sectors. Whether it is an industry, financial institution, educational institute, home, or office, security has become an absolute necessity.

Our Services are built around our core desire to successfully meet and exceed our clients' expectations. Our experienced team, coupled with our innovative and resourceful personnel, is fully competent to endure any challenge — delivering proactive protection rather than a reactive response.
